ShackBrowse Privacy Policy

The ShackBrowse app is primarily a reader/posting app for ShackNews.com. It is an unofficial app and is not developed, distributed or endorsed by ShackNews. This is a volunteer driven app and is open source.

You should review the ShackNews Privacy Policy first as ShackBrowse primarily reads and posts to ShackNews like any other browser or app:

Shacknews Privacy Policy

Photos or videos posted through the Imgur functionality are open to the Internet and should be treated as such. See Imgur's Privacy Policy at:

Imgur Privacy Policy

Photos & Videos are not stored locally beyond default Android behaviour and is not transmitted to any other system. Imgur is the only active photo/video integration in the app.

Shackbrowse Specific Privacy Policy

No information outside of the below information will be shared with any other party. This app is maintained by unpaid volunteers and we have no intent or desire to share any private information with any other party.

The ShackBrowse app itself will only store your Username and Password if you enter it for authentication. It will store this information so it can authenticate and post as your user. This information is not shared with any other outside service or party.

Google Firebase Crashlytics is integrated into the ShackBrowse application for basic crash and debug analytics usage. Only basic crash analytics is gathered and no username, IP, etc or other identifying information is gathered. The phone model itself is part of crash analytics but it does not log anything unique to the phone.

Google Firebase Analytics is integrated into the ShackBrowse application for basic analytics data like number of sessions worldwide, time spent in Android views, etc. No user identifying data is tied to the analytics, only the default configuration of Firebase Analytics is used.
